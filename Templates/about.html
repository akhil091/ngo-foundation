About us

PREVENT THE CURABLE BLINDNESS

Halocare foundation is a non- profit organization started in 2020 by Dr. Anuj Chauhan and his young doctor team. Halocare foundation is committed to provide comprehensive, affordable/costless, and quality eye care services to the neediest and to make India a better nation with complete eye care for all citizens.

Our foundation organizes outreach camps every week in rural and semi-urban areas to raise awareness, screen and treat eye defects and ailments. By offering extensive visual health services, we aim to offer the best possible treatment to all of the communities.

We are planning to set up a free eye clinic for screening as well as a reference center for cataract surgery at grass root level or block level and also have one price clinic that will be affordable to everyone for eye care, medical care, contact glasses, lenses, medicines, and eye drops.

Our mission

Our mission is to fight against curable and preventable blindness in India. To reduce the number of visually impaired people in India by providing low-cost or no-cost eye care for the underprivileged through creative eye care models across the state.

Our aim

Our main aim is to raise more funds to assist more people in need of medical care. To reach out to more people to raise awareness about their eye health and prevent cataract-related blindness, we will create eye screening centers at the block level to reach more people.

Our vision

The idea and need of a Halocare foundation were born out of the necessity of providing vision to all sections of the rural and urban population who are unable to access any primary eye care. Our vision is focused on delivering the best possible care for our patients.

Our Services

We Believe that We Can Save More Lifes with You

Free eye drops and contact glasses

Free eye drops and contact glasses to the needy at our eye camps. We offer premium glasses and medicine at affordable prices.

Eye Camps

We conduct free eye camps every week at one village. We Will cover one village in one week to screen everyone needy.

Medicines

We are distributing free medicines, masks and sanitizer to the needful at the grassroot level as well as we are sanitizing the villages during COVID-19 pandemic.

Free cataract surgery and check-ups

We provide free cataract surgery at top-notch charitable hospital in Delhi NCR. We provide free check-ups after cataract surgery and eye drops at low prices till the patient's recovery.
